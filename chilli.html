About the Product Guntur chilli hails from the Guntur District in Andhra Pradesh, India. Famous for its fiery heat and flavour, it's highly favoured by spice enthusiasts around the globe. These chillies are primarily utilised in the creation of chutneys, curries, and powders. Guntur chillies are available in a variety of hues, including white, yellow, and red. The Guntur region stands as the largest producer and exporter of most varieties of chillies and chilli powder in India.
